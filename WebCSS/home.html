Pune Unveiled

Explore the Heartbeat of Maharashtra!

Shaniwar Wada

Explore the historical heart of Pune at Shaniwar Wada, a majestic fort built in the 18th century. Marvel at its architectural splendor, delve into its rich history, and witness the light and sound show that brings the past to life.

Explore

Aga Khan Palace

Visit the Aga Khan Palace, a poignant symbol of India's independence movement. This architectural gem houses a museum showcasing memorabilia from the freedom struggle and serves as a serene retreat with beautiful gardens.

Explore

Sinhagad Fort

For a taste of adventure and panoramic views, embark on a journey to Sinhagad Fort. A short drive from Pune, this hill fortress offers a blend of history and natural beauty, making it a favorite trekking destination among locals and tourists alike.

Cultural Melting Pot

Pune, the "Oxford of the East," blends tradition and modernity with a rich cultural mix.

Historical Significance

As the former seat of the Peshwas, Pune's history is steeped in Maratha valor.

Green Tech Hub

Pune thrives as an IT hub, yet maintains harmony with nature. Boasting lush greenery and a focus on sustainability.
